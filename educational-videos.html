Educational Videos

Explore our educational videos on palliative care, designed to support patients and families with compassionate guidance and practical insights.

Need for tender conversations in palliative care - Dr. Mhoira E F Leng

Dr. Mhoira Leng is DGHS PCEC, a palliative care physician and senior advisor at Palliative Care Medicine University of Edinburgh and Mulago Hospital. She is the medical director of Cairdeas International Palliative Care Trust, research coordinator for PallCHASE, and a senior fellow at the University of Edinburgh's Global Health Academy.

Communication: Values, skills and attitude - Dr. Chitra Venkateswaran

Dr. Chitra Venkateswaran, HOD, Department of Psychiatry and Palliative Care, Believers Church Medical College Hospital. She's Founder/ Clinical Director, Mehac Foundation, and a Board member at the International Association of Hospice and Palliative Care.

Need for self care among health care professionals - Dr. Chitra Venkateswaran

Dr. Chitra Venkateswaran highlights the evolving nature of communication in palliative care, with emphasizing empathy, active listening, and personalized compassionate conversations to improve patient experience and outcomes.

Self care; compassion & humanity - Dr Mhoira E F Leng

Dr. Mhoira Leng delivered a talk on "Caring for Others, Compassion, and Humanity" as part of the academic program organized by the Department of Palliative Medicine at CMC Vellore on October 3, 2022.

Palliative Care in Nepal: Experiences and Lessons Learnt - Dr Daniel Munday

Dr Daniel Munday, a stalwart in palliative care, shares his journey in Nepal, where he has done extensive work in integrating palliative care in chronic illness management within rural hospital and community settings.
