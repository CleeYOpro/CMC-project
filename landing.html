Who We Are

The Department of Palliative Medicine at Christian Medical College Vellore is dedicated to offering compassionate, whole-person care for patients with advanced cancers and chronic conditions like COPD, ILD, and renal failure. Our caring team of doctors, nurses, psychologists, social workers, and chaplains works closely with patients and their families to provide comfort, support, and hope through every step of their journey.
